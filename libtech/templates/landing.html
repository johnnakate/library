about LibTech

CIT-U Library Reservations System is a web and mobile based system that will allow students to view the remaining available seats and discussion rooms inside the CIT-U college library. This aims to minimize overcrowding inside the library, especially when the exam period comes you can choose your spot for the discussion rooms conveniently. A reservation ensures that the room is saved for the student and cannot be taken by another patron during the said scheduled time.
